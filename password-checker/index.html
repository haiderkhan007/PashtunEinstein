5 Tips for Creating Strong Passwords

  1. Use at least 16 characters. Longer passwords are exponentially harder to crack.
  2. Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
  3. Never reuse passwords across different accounts.
  4. Avoid personal information like names, birthdays, or common words.
  5. Use a password manager to store unique passwords for every account.
