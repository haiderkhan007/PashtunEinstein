About the SecureText AES Encryption Tool

The internet is not always safe. When your data is moving from one place to another (in transit), hackers, systems, or trackers may try to read it. This AES Encryption Tool protects your communication by converting your message into ciphertext — a coded form that looks like random text.

Only someone with the correct key (password) can turn the ciphertext back into the original readable message. This keeps your private communication safe even if someone intercepts your data.

How to Use

  1. Type your message in the text box.
  2. Create a strong secret key (password).
  3. Press Encrypt to convert your message into ciphertext.
  4. Send the encrypted text to anyone using email, WhatsApp, Messenger, etc.
  5. Important: Do NOT send the key with the encrypted message. Share the key separately (for example: phone call).

When the other person receives the ciphertext, they can paste it into this tool and enter the correct key to decrypt it. The tool will then return the original message.

You can also use this tool to encrypt PDFs, documents, notes, or any text-based file. It is extremely useful for privacy-conscious users, journalists, professionals, and anyone who wants secure communication.
